Problem 2(write)

Capture A "Firstname Lastname" name at the beginning of each line.
e.g. for sentence
Jane Doe is eating breakfast.
Your regex should capture "Jane Doe".
But in sentence
Today is John Doe's birthday.
Your regex should NOT capture the name in the sentence as it does not start from the beginning of the string.
Notice that the name should consist of two words, each word has at least one letter, and starts with a capital letter. No other symbols are allowed in the name.
Some valid names are: Issac Newton, I Newton;
Some invalid names are: john doe, Ling-Ling Zhang.
