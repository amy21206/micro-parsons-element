Regular Expression Practice

Thank you for participating in the study in practicing regex. The purpose of this study is to evaluate different tools we developed for practicing regex problems. In this session, you will:
  1. Go through a tutorial of the regex tools(~5 miutes)
  2. Answer 4 regex problems with the tools.(less than 7 minutes each)
  3. Fill out a short questionnaire about the tool.(~3 minutes)
Please make sure you complete the tasks in an uninterrupted session and do not refresh the page, or your progress will be lost and it will not be marked as completed.

During the process, your interaction data with the tool will be collected.
If you agree in participating the study, please fill in your student number(UMID), name, and email address below. Then, click "Confirm" to continue.
